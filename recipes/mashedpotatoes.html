Mashed Potatoes

Description

How to cook a buttery, creamy mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds potatoes
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into evenly sized chunks.
  2. Place the potatoes into a large stockpot of salted cold water and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-high and continue cooking for about 10-12 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the middle of a potato goes in easily with almost no resistance. Carefully drain out all of the water.
  4. Heat the butter, milk and an additional 2 teaspoons of sea salt together either in a small saucepan or in the microwave until the butter is just melted.
  5. Mash the potatoes and fold in the butter mixture until just combined.
